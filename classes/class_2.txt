Class: Menu

Attributes:
dishes(array);
price(float);
happyHour(bool);
breakfastHours(string);

Methods:
addNewDish(dishes.push("new dish")) (this will modify the dishes array to add a dish)
changePrice(price = y) (this will modify the price attribute)
applyHappyHour(happyHour = true) (this will modify the happyHour bool to true)
changeBreakfastHours(breakfastHours = "new hours") (this will modify the breakfast hours on the menu)
